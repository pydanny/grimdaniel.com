## Progress on the sequel to "Everyone Dies"

While I've been shaping the final edges of "Everyone Dies" for its release on Amazon on May 20th, I've also I’ve also begun carving into its sequel. This is the story about the cost of survival, where the coin is betrayal and murder . If "Everyone Dies" was about, well, everyone dying, the sequel is what happens when someone is willing to spend other people's lives so they can keep on living.

One angle that will be included is the non-destructive element of necromancy. Specifically, how a necropolitan society might use the undead to perform hard labor, thus making the life of the living easier. Then the question arises of how over time would the labor ranks of undead be filled, which is answered that it would be a punishment. This topic will be covered in the sequel to "Everyone Dies".
