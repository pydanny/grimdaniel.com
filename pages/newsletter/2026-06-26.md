In this edition of the **Not Dead Yet** newsletter, I lead with a link to the upcoming sequel to Everyone Dies plus a mention to the beta readers club. I also mention my review of Z.B. Steele's "Whispers of the Storm". 


## [First Chapter of Soldier of the Death God](/books/soldier-of-the-death-god)

If you want to read the still rough first chapter of the sequel to "Everyone Dies", you can find it on the new [Soldier of the Death God](/books/soldier-of-the-death-god) page on my website.

Also, the [Soldier of the Death God](/books/soldier-of-the-death-god) page links to my **beta readers club**. Sign up to get new chapters as I finish writing them. Yup, I'm opening the door for readers to give me early constructive feedback on my next book.
