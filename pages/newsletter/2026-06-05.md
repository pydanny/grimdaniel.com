## Everyone Dies post-launch report

On May 20th I released my first new fiction book in over six years, the grimdark novella "Everyone Dies", on Amazon. Here's how it did in terms of sales:

- 11 Preorders
- 21 Paid Orders
- 128 Kindle Unlimited Pages Read (I subtracted all three times I re-read the book)
- 198 Free Orders

As for reviews, it did quite well, although I wouldn't mind more of them:

- <a href="https://storyoriginapp.com/universalbooklinks/8c487a18-2b3e-11f1-aa1d-dfa5b3422a69" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Amazon: 4.5 stars on 6 reviews</a>
- <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/250190086-everyone-dies" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Goodreads: 4.36 stars on 11 reviews</a> (FYI, I'm obnoxious and self-voted 5 stars)

While sales are far from enough to allow me to quit my dayjob, getting the book out was an invaluable learning experience. I revisited how to format books and explored the new and different world of author promotion.
