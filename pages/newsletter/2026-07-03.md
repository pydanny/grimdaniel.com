## Slowing down the newsletter

While it's fun to write the "Not Dead Yet" Newsletter, it does take time away from my fiction writing. And since [Everyone Dies](https://storyoriginapp.com/universalbooklinks/8c487a18-2b3e-11f1-aa1d-dfa5b3422a69) has been out for about 45 days now, I think it's time to really dig into crafting new stories. Once I get closer to releasing my next book I'll make this posting more frequent again.
