<p>Hey there,</p>
<p>I'm not dead yet. Unlike the unfortunate characters in my stories, I'm alive and writing. As you may know, I write fantasy in the grimdark genre.</p>

<p>I've been asked how many words I write in a day. My average is around 3,500 words per day. The editing process will remove abou 10-20% of those.</p>
