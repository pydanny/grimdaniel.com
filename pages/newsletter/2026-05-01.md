<p>Hey there,</p>
<p>I'm not dead yet. Unlike the unfortunate characters in my stories, I'm alive and writing. As you may know, I write fantasy in the grimdark genre.</p>

<p>I've been asked how many words I write in a day. My average is around 3,500 words per day. The grammar cleanup process removes 10-20%, but editing counters that as dialogue and description is expanded to make the story flow better. I would like to get up to 4,000 words a day, but life often gets in the way.</p>
