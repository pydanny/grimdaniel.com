<p>Hey there,</p>
<p>I'm not dead yet. Unlike the unfortunate characters in my stories, I'm alive and writing. As you may know, I write fantasy in the grimdark genre.</p>
<p>What you may not know is I love feedback. Hearing from allows me to grow as an author, to be more successul at it. Don't hold back your punches, give it to me like the hapless victim of a grimdark protagonist.</p>

<h2 class="ql-align-center">Everyone Dies Sequel Title Poll</h2>
<p>The sequel to "<strong>Everyone Dies</strong>" is starting to take shape. This is the story about the cost of survival, where the coin is betrayal and murder . If "<strong>Everyone Dies</strong>" was about, well, everyone dying, the sequel is how someone is willing to spend other people's lives so they can keep on living.</p>

<p>I'll admit to being stuck on a title. "Everyone Dies 2" could work, but I've got ideas for other names. Or at least, the secondary title. If the first book "Seven companions walk a path no one survives", the follow up should have its own delineating subtitle.</p>
