## Everyone Dies free until midnight May 26th

As you can see from the banner image, Everyone Dies is <a href="https://storyoriginapp.com/universalbooklinks/8c487a18-2b3e-11f1-aa1d-dfa5b3422a69" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">free on Amazon until midnight on May 26th</a>. If you aren't on Kindle Unlimited and want it for free, here's your chance!

## Reviews are coming in and they are positive!

The feedback from readers has been positive, resulting in a <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/250190086-everyone-dies"  rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">4.17-star average on Goodreads</a>. Goodreads reviewers are often much more critical than Amazon readers, so I'm really pleased by this rating.

As for Amazon reviews, those haven't appeared yet. A few of you have told me that you submitted there, but as of this email, they aren't yet approved. Usually, it takes Amazon anywhere from 4-24 hours to approve reviews, but I've heard sometimes it takes four or five days.


## Grimdark Popcorn!

This weekend, my seven-year-old daughter, <strong>Uma</strong>, is running a booth in a kid's entrepreneurial event in Manila called "Tiny Tycoons". She's selling "popcorn with prizes". She came up with the product, brand, and logo. She even has a parent-curated <a href="https://www.instagram.com/pripopz" target="_blank">Instagram account</a> and <a href="https://pripopz.com" target="_blank">website</a> for it.

While Uma is too young to read Everyone Dies, she's excited about it in the way seven-year-olds get excited about things. So for her popcorn booth, she's created a limited edition "grimdark" flavor  to celebrate my book launch, a mix of black pepper, edible charcoal dust, and sesame seeds cooked in sesame oil.
