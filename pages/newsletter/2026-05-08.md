<p>Hey there,</p>

<p>I'm not dead yet. Unlike the unfortunate characters in my stories, I'm alive and writing. As you may know, I write fantasy in the grimdark genre.</p>

## News

I am at the quarter mark for first draft to the sequel to Everyone Dies. To ensure enough time for editing, I am considering moving the publication date to July.

## Personal Note

As someone who tries to have a positive outlook on life, sometimes I wonder why my fiction writings tend to be dark. It could be an outlet for negative emotions. Perhaps by writing about negative worlds and events I am able to extract those bad feelings from my person in a constructive way.

However, that doesn't explain why I like to read about dark settings, characters, and situations. If I ever figure that out, I'll share it here.
