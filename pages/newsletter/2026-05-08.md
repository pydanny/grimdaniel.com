## News

The first reviews for "Everyone Dies" are now showing up on <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/250190086-everyone-dies" target="_blank">goodreads</a>. Of course I want five stars all the way. Yet even ratings that aren't perfect are useful in that they can provide feedback for improving future wring efforts.

I am at the quarter mark for first draft to the sequel to Everyone Dies. To ensure enough time for editing, I am considering moving the publication date to July.

## Personal Note

As someone who tries to have a positive outlook on life, sometimes I wonder why my fiction writings tend to be dark. I suppose it is an outlet for negative emotions. Perhaps by writing about negative worlds I am able to extract those bad feelings from my person in a constructive way.

However, that doesn't explain why I like to read about dark settings, characters, and events. If I ever figure that out, I'll share it here.
